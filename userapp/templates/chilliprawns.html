    • Chilli Prawns

    Chilli Prawns are a spicy and savory dish featuring prawns cooked in a tangy chili sauce. This popular Asian-inspired recipe combines the rich flavors of garlic, ginger, and soy sauce with a hint of sweetness.

    Ingredients

    • 250g prawns, cleaned and deveined
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), thinly sliced
    • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
    • 1 teaspoon vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • Salt to taste
    • Chopped spring onions for garnish

    Instructions

    1.

    Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.

    2.

    Add sliced onion, bell pepper, and green chilies. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender.

    3.

    Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant.

    4.

    Add cleaned prawns to the pan and stir-fry until they turn pink and opaque.

    5.

    In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Pour this sauce over the prawns and vegetables.

    6.

    Stir well to coat the prawns and vegetables with the sauce. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.

    7.

    Garnish with chopped spring onions.

    8.

    Serve hot as an appetizer or as a side dish with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy your delicious chilli prawns!
