    • Butter Chicken

    Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy dish made with tender chicken pieces in a flavorful tomato-based sauce. It is a beloved dish in Indian cuisine, known for its vibrant color and aromatic spices, and is often enjoyed with naan or rice.

    Ingredients

    • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 cup cream
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

    Instructions

    1.

    Heat oil and butter in a pan over medium heat.

    2.

    Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

    3.

    Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

    4.

    Add chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they turn white and are no longer pink.

    5.

    Add chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.

    6.

    Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.

    7.

    Add cream and garam masala. Mix well and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the gravy thickens.

    8.

    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

    9.

    Serve hot with naan or rice. Enjoy your creamy and delicious butter chicken!
