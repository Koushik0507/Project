    • Samosa

    A delicious and classic dish that is perfect for any meal. This samosa recipe combines crispy pastry with a flavorful filling, making it a favorite among food lovers.

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Water for kneading dough
    • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
    • 1 cup boiled peas
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1 teaspoon amchur (dried mango powder) or lemon juice
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (optional)
    • Oil for frying

    Instructions

    1.

    In a bowl, mix flour, oil, and salt. Add water gradually and knead to make a firm dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.

    2.

    Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.

    3.

    Add grated ginger and sauté for a minute.

    4.

    Add mashed potatoes, boiled peas, garam masala, coriander powder, amchur, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add chopped coriander leaves. Let the mixture cool.

    5.

    Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin oval-shaped sheet.

    6.

    Cut the rolled dough into halves. Take one half, apply water on the straight edge, and fold it to form a cone.

    7.

    Fill the cone with the potato-peas mixture and seal the open edges tightly.

    8.

    Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.

    9.

    Once the oil is hot, gently slide in the samosas and fry on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy.

    10.

    Remove from oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.

    11.

    Serve hot with green chutney or ketchup. Enjoy your delicious samosas!
