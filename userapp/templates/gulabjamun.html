    • Gulab Jamun

    A delicious and classic dish that is perfect for any meal. This samosa recipe combines crispy pastry with a flavorful filling, making it a favorite among food lovers.

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup milk powder
    • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
    • 2 tablespoons ghee or melted unsalted butter
    • 1/4 cup milk, or as needed to make a dough
    • Oil or ghee for frying
    • For sugar syrup: - 1 cup sugar - 1 cup water - 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder - A few saffron strands (optional) - A few drops of rose water (optional)

    Instructions

    1.

    In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking powder.

    2.

    Add ghee or melted butter and mix well.

    3.

    Gradually add milk and knead into a soft dough. Add more milk if needed.

    4.

    Divide the dough into small equal-sized portions and roll them into smooth balls without any cracks.

    5.

    Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan over low-medium heat.

    6.

    Once the oil is hot, carefully add the rolled dough balls and fry on low heat until they turn golden brown. Stir gently to ensure even frying.

    7.

    Remove the fried balls using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on a paper towel.

    8.

    In another saucepan, prepare the sugar syrup by combining sugar, water, cardamom powder, saffron strands (if using), and rose water (if using). Bring to a boil and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the syrup thickens slightly.

    9.

    Turn off the heat and add the fried balls to the warm sugar syrup.

    10.

    Let the gulab jamuns soak in the syrup for at least 1-2 hours before serving.

    11.

    Serve warm or at room temperature. Enjoy your delicious homemade gulab jamuns!
