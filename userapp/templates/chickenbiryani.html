    • Chicken Biryani

    Chicken Biryani is a popular dish known for its rich aroma and delightful flavors. This recipe creates a classic biryani with a blend of spices and tender chicken.

    Ingredients

    • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
    • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
    • 1 onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 tomato, chopped
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
    • Fresh coriander leaves and fried onions for garnish (optional)

    Instructions

    1.

    Heat oil or ghee in a pressure cooker or heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.

    2.

    Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.

    3.

    Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

    4.

    Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white and are no longer pink.

    5.

    Add chopped tomatoes, garam masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.

    6.

    Add yogurt and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the chicken is tender and the masala is well blended.

    7.

    Add soaked and drained rice, spreading it evenly over the chicken and masala. Pour water over the rice until it is completely submerged.

    8.

    Close the lid and cook on medium heat for one whistle (if using a pressure cooker) or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.

    9.

    Let the pressure release naturally, then fluff the biryani gently with a fork.

    10.

    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and fried onions if desired.

    11.

    Serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your flavorful chicken biryani!
