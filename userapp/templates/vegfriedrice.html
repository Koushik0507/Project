    • Veg Fried Rice

    Veg Fried Rice is a popular Asian-inspired dish made with cooked rice and a mix of colorful vegetables. Typically, the rice is stir-fried with onions, bell peppers, carrots, peas, and other vegetables, often seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover and cooled)
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.), diced
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 2 green onions, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon chilli sauce (optional)
    • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • Salt & Pepper to taste
    • Spring onions for garnish (optional)

    Instructions

    1.

    Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.

    2.

    Add minced garlic and diced onion. Sauté until onions turn translucent.

    3.

    Add diced mixed vegetables and cook until they are slightly tender but still crisp.

    4.

    Add cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps with a spoon.

    5.

    Pour soy sauce, chili sauce (if using), vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper over the rice and vegetables. Stir well to combine.

    6.

    Cook for another 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until everything is heated through.

    7.

    Garnish with chopped green onions (spring onions) if desired.

    8.

    Serve hot as a main dish or as a side with your favorite Asian-inspired meal. Enjoy your delicious veg friedrice!
