    • Veg Biryani

    Veg Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with a variety of vegetables and basmati rice, infused with fragrant spices like saffron, cardamom, and cloves. Often cooked in layers with a mix of vegetables, rice, and herbs.

    Ingredients

    • 1 onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 tomato, chopped
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, cauliflower, etc.)
    • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 2-3 green cardamom pods
    • 2-3 cloves
    • 1-inch cinnamon stick
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
    • Coriander leaves for garnish
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 cups water
    • Fresh coriander leaves and fried onions for garnish (optional)

    Instructions

    1.

    Heat oil or ghee in a pressure cooker or heavy-bottomed pot.

    2.

    Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.

    3.

    Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.

    4.

    Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.

    5.

    Add mixed vegetables, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.

    6.

    Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Stir gently to coat the rice with the masala.

    7.

    Add water and mix well. Bring to a boil.

    8.

    Close the lid of the pressure cooker or pot and cook on medium heat for one whistle (if using a pressure cooker) or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.

    9.

    Once done, let the pressure release naturally before opening the lid.

    10.

    Fluff the biryani gently with a fork.

    11.

    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and fried onions if desired.

    12.

    Serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your flavorful veg biryani!
