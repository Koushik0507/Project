    • Chilli Paneer

    Chilli Paneer is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that features cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a spicy and tangy chili sauce. This vegetarian dish is a favorite at Indian restaurants and is known for its bold flavors and crispy texture.

    Ingredients

    • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
    • 1 onion, diced
    • 1 capsicum (bell pepper), diced
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon chilli sauce
    • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
    • 1 teaspoon vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon cornflour
    • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for frying
    • Spring onions for garnish (optional)

    Instructions

    1.

    In a bowl, mix soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, and salt to make a marinade.

    2.

    Marinate paneer cubes in the mixture for 15-20 minutes.

    3.

    Heat oil in a pan, add marinated paneer cubes, and fry until golden brown. Remove and set aside.

    4.

    In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed. Add diced onion, capsicum, and green chilies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

    5.

    Add the fried paneer cubes to the pan and toss everything together.

    6.

    Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

    7.

    Garnish with chopped spring onions if desired.

    8.

    Serve hot with fried rice or noodles. Enjoy your flavorful chilli paneer!
