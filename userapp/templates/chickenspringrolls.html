    • Chicken Spring Roll

    Chicken Spring Rolls are a popular Asian appetizer made with thin wrappers filled with a savory mixture of chicken, vegetables, and spices. These rolls are typically deep-fried to a crispy golden brown and are often served with a variety of dipping sauces.

    Ingredients

    • 200g boneless chicken, finely chopped or shredded
    • 1 cup shredded cabbage
    • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
    • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
    • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Spring roll wrappers
    • Oil for frying

    Instructions

    1.

    Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

    2.

    Add chopped chicken to the pan and cook until it turns white and is no longer pink.

    3.

    Add shredded cabbage, carrots, and bell peppers to the pan. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

    4.

    Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.

    5.

    Remove the filling from the heat and let it cool slightly.

    6.

    Take a spring roll wrapper and place a spoonful of the chicken and vegetable filling in the center.

    7.

    Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then roll it tightly into a cylinder, sealing the edge with water.

    8.

    Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.

    9.

    Once the oil is hot, carefully place the spring rolls in the oil and fry until they are golden brown and crispy.

    10.

    Remove the spring rolls from the oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.

    11.

    Serve hot with sweet chili sauce or your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy your delicious chicken spring rolls!
